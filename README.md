# 🧠 WojakMeter

**The Crypto Emotion Index**

WojakMeter is a real-time crypto sentiment platform that transforms market data into something traders actually understand:

👉 **emotion**

Instead of just numbers and charts,  WojakMeter answers:

> *What does the market feel like right now?*

## 🌡️ The 7 Emotional States

WojakMeter translates market conditions into **7 emotional stages**, forming a complete psychological cycle:

| Emotion        | Range | Meaning |
|--------------|------|--------|
| 🔴 Frustration | 0–19 | Capitulation, exhaustion, emotional selling |
| 🔴 Concern     | 20–34 | Fear building, uncertainty rising |
| 🟠 Doubt       | 35–44 | Indecision, weak conviction |
| ⚪ Neutral     | 45–59 | Balance, no clear direction |
| 🟢 Optimism    | 60–69 | Confidence forming, early strength |
| 🟢 Content     | 70–84 | Stable uptrend, comfort in positions |
| 🟢 Euphoria    | 85–100 | Overconfidence, peak emotional state |

This scale mirrors real market cycles:

> Fear → Recovery → Confidence → Euphoria → Reset
